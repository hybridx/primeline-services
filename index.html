Frequently Asked Questions

What payment services do you support?

We accept PayTM, Google Pay, Amazon Pay, UPI Payments etc.

Will the promised speed be consistent, no matter where I browse?

The overall browser performance will be dictated by the screen size, the distance between the Smart Router and the device (say, your computer) and number of devices connected to the internet at the same time. So, it may be possible that the same connection will serve 2-3 different speeds depending on the above-mentioned factors.

What does a month refer to ?

A month refers to a period of 30 Days.

Do the speed-testers measure the speed of my connection?

Nearly all speed testing apps do not measure the speed but instead measure the bandwidth (capacity). Unfortunately, the bandwidth is the measure of how much data can the connection hold and not the speed (throughput) of the connection. It's only the speed that governs the user app experience in most cases.
